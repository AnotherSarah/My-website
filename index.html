About this website

I have made this website in order to share a few things I am proud of. Creating can sometimes be a hard process, so having a place to remind yourself of what you are capable to achieve can help get inspired. And if that place is on the internet, you might even have a chance to inspire someone else! So now, if you feel like it, take a minute to get lost on my page.
